Business Strategies for Deriv Partners

Deriv offers flexible partnership opportunities suited to different business models and areas of expertise. This guide will help you understand the various business strategies and promotion channels you can use to grow your Deriv partnership.

Important: The key decision when joining Deriv Partners is choosing your commission model, not a specific business strategy. You have the flexibility to use any promotion method or combination of methods that work best for your business.

Promotion Channels Overview

As a Deriv partner, you can use various promotion channels to attract and retain clients. Most successful partners use a combination of these approaches:

Digital Marketing

Promote Deriv through websites, blogs, social media, content marketing, SEO, email campaigns, and online advertising.

Direct Relationships

Leverage personal connections, network with traders, provide personalized support, and maintain ongoing relationships with your clients.

Technical Solutions

Develop trading applications, signals services, or analysis tools using Deriv API to provide added value to traders.

Flexible Approach to Partnership

Rather than being limited to a single approach, we encourage partners to adopt a hybrid strategy that combines different methods to maximize their success:

  • Multi-channel promotion: Use both online marketing and direct client relationships
  • Tailored approaches: Adapt your strategy based on different target markets or client segments
  • Strategic combinations: For example, use content marketing for broad reach while providing personalized support to high-value clients
Pro Tip: The key to success is selecting the right commission model for your business strategy and target audience.

Effective Business Strategies

Here are some proven business strategies that our partners use successfully:

Digital Marketing Strategy

This strategy focuses on reaching a wide audience through online channels:

  • Create a website, blog, or social media presence focused on trading or financial topics
  • Develop valuable content that educates traders and showcases Deriv's benefits
  • Optimize your content for search engines (SEO) to attract organic traffic
  • Use email marketing to nurture leads and maintain contact with potential clients
  • Run targeted online ad campaigns to reach specific trader demographics
  • Create instructional videos or webinars about trading on Deriv platforms
Best suited for: Digital marketers, content creators, SEO specialists, bloggers, social media influencers, and online communities in the finance or trading space.

Client Relationship Strategy

This strategy focuses on building and maintaining personal connections with traders:

  • Leverage your existing network of traders or potential traders
  • Provide personalized guidance and support to your clients
  • Offer trading education, analysis, or signals as added value
  • Host webinars, workshops, or in-person events for traders
  • Develop a reputation as a trusted advisor in the trading community
  • Focus on client retention and satisfaction to build long-term relationships
Best suited for: Trading educators, signal providers, financial advisors, trading community leaders, and professionals with established networks in trading circles.

Technical Solution Strategy

This strategy involves creating technical tools or applications that add value for traders:

  • Develop custom trading applications or platforms using Deriv's API
  • Create trading bots, indicators, or analysis tools
  • Provide technical solutions that simplify trading or enhance trader results
  • Build mobile apps or browser extensions that integrate with Deriv
  • Offer premium technical tools with Deriv integration
  • Leverage your technical expertise to attract and retain traders
Best suited for: Developers, FinTech companies, technical analysts, and trading system creators who can build software solutions for traders.

Choosing the Right Commission Model

Your business strategy should inform your choice of commission model. Here's a guide to help you align your strategy with the most suitable model:

Business Strategy Recommended Commission Model Reason
Digital marketing with broad audience Consider both models based on product focus The best model depends on which products your audience is most likely to trade
Digital marketing for CFD traders Turnover Commission Maximizes earnings from CFD trading volume
Digital marketing for derivatives traders Revenue Share Potentially higher earnings from derivatives trading
Client relationships with high-volume traders Turnover Commission Rewards high trading volume regardless of profitability
Client relationships with derivatives traders Revenue Share Higher percentage rates for active derivatives traders
Technical solutions for CFD trading Turnover Commission Aligns with CFD trading focus
Technical solutions for derivatives trading Revenue Share Better rewards for derivatives trading activity
Focus on EU markets CPA (available for EU clients only) Provides fixed payment per qualified EU client

Learn more about each model on our Commission models page.

Selecting Your Business Approach

When deciding on your business strategy and commission model, consider these factors:

  • Your expertise and resources: Choose strategies that leverage your existing skills and available resources
  • Your target audience: Consider who you can reach most effectively and what products they're likely to trade
  • Your competitive advantage: Focus on areas where you have unique strengths or positioning
  • Scalability: Consider how your strategy can grow over time as your partnership develops
  • Time investment: Assess how much time you can dedicate to different promotional activities
  • Long-term sustainability: Choose approaches that can provide sustainable results over time
Still deciding on your approach? You can start with one strategy and expand or adjust as you learn what works best for your business. Many successful partners began with a focused approach and gradually diversified their strategies.
