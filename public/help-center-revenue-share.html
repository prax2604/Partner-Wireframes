Revenue Share Commission Model

The Revenue Share model is a powerful commission structure that aligns your earnings directly with Deriv's revenue. Under this model, you earn a percentage of the net revenue generated from your referred clients' trading activities, creating a true partnership where your success is tied to ours.

Important: Revenue Share provides the highest earning potential for partners whose clients generate significant profits for Deriv, particularly with options and multiplier products.

Key Benefits of Revenue Share

Higher Earning Potential

Earn up to 45% of the net revenue generated by your clients, which can result in significantly higher commissions compared to other models.

Long-term Value

Benefit from the lifetime value of your clients as long as they continue trading, creating a sustainable income stream.

Tiered Commission Rates

Increase your earnings with our progressive commission structure that rewards partners who bring in higher net revenue.

How Revenue Share Works

The Revenue Share model calculates your commission based on the net revenue Deriv earns from your referred clients' trading activities. Here's a simplified explanation of the process:

  1. You refer clients to Deriv through your affiliate links
  2. Clients register and start trading on Deriv platforms
  3. Deriv calculates the net revenue generated from these clients (total revenue minus costs)
  4. You receive a percentage of this net revenue according to your tier level
Note: Net revenue is calculated as the gross profit from client trading activity minus applicable costs such as payment processing fees, fraud-related chargebacks, and promotional costs.

Commission Rates

Our Revenue Share commission structure operates on a tiered system based on the monthly net revenue generated by your referred clients:

Monthly Net Revenue Commission Rate Partner Tier
$20,000 or less 30% Standard
Over $20,000 45% Premium
Pro Tip: Focus on attracting traders who are likely to be active on derivatives products (options and multipliers) as these typically generate higher net revenue compared to CFD products.

Factors Affecting Revenue Share Earnings

Several factors can influence your earnings under the Revenue Share model:

Client Trading Behavior

  • Trading frequency: More active clients typically generate more revenue
  • Product selection: Different products have different profit margins for Deriv
  • Trading success: If clients consistently win, Deriv's revenue (and your commission) decreases

Market Conditions

  • Volatility: Higher market volatility often leads to more trading activity
  • Major economic events: Can influence trading patterns and outcomes

Client Retention

  • Long-term activity: Clients who trade for longer periods provide more consistent revenue
  • Redepositing behavior: Clients who regularly fund their accounts tend to generate more revenue

Comparison with Other Models

Different commission models suit different partner strategies and client types:

Revenue Share vs. Turnover Commission

  • Revenue Share: Based on the profit Deriv makes from clients; potentially higher earnings but more variable.
  • Turnover Commission: Based on trading volume regardless of outcome; more predictable but potentially lower earnings.

Revenue Share vs. CPA

  • Revenue Share: Ongoing commissions based on client activity; unlimited long-term earning potential.
  • CPA: One-time payment per client acquisition; immediate returns but limited earning potential.
