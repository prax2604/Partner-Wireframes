Frequently Asked Questions

What marketing materials can I access as a Deriv partner?

As a Deriv partner, you have access to a comprehensive range of marketing materials, including:

  • Banner ads in various sizes and formats
  • Landing page templates
  • Email marketing templates
  • Social media posts and graphics
  • Promotional videos and animations
  • Product information sheets
  • Educational content for your audience

All materials are regularly updated and can be accessed through your partner dashboard.

Can I customize the marketing materials with my own branding?

Yes, many of our marketing materials can be customized to include your branding elements while maintaining Deriv's brand integrity. The Banner Generator and Landing Page tools allow for custom color schemes, logo placement, and messaging tailored to your audience.

However, certain brand elements of Deriv must remain unchanged to comply with our brand guidelines. Please refer to the Brand Guidelines document for specific details on what can and cannot be modified.

Are there any restrictions on how I can market Deriv products?

Yes, there are regulatory and compliance guidelines that all partners must follow when marketing Deriv products:

  • All marketing materials must be accurate and not misleading
  • Risk warnings must be prominently displayed
  • No guarantees of profit or unrealistic claims should be made
  • Marketing to minors or in restricted jurisdictions is prohibited
  • Specific regulatory requirements may apply based on your target market

We recommend reviewing our Compliance Guidelines for detailed information on marketing restrictions.

How do I ensure my marketing links track conversions correctly?

To ensure proper tracking of your marketing efforts:

  1. Always use the Tracking Link Builder or Banner Generator from your partner dashboard to create links
  2. Make sure your affiliate ID is correctly included in all links
  3. Use different campaign IDs for different marketing channels or campaigns to track performance separately
  4. Regularly check your partner dashboard to verify that conversions are being tracked
  5. If you notice any discrepancies, contact our partner support team immediately

Our tracking cookies have a 90-day duration, so clients who click your links will be attributed to your account if they sign up within that period.

How often are new marketing materials added?

We regularly update our marketing materials library with new content. New materials are typically added:

  • Monthly for standard promotional materials
  • For special events, market opportunities, and seasonal campaigns
  • When new products or features are launched
  • In response to partner feedback and requests

We recommend checking the Marketing Tools section of your partner dashboard weekly for the latest materials. You can also subscribe to our partner newsletter to be notified when significant new materials are available.
