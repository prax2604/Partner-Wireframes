Frequently asked questions about commissions

Why are CFD commissions different from options commissions?

CFD trading operates on the Deriv MT5 platform and involves different market mechanisms than options trading. For CFDs, we offer turnover-based commissions, which provide a fixed rate per lot traded regardless of client profitability.

Options trading uses our proprietary platforms, allowing us to offer revenue share models where you earn a percentage of client net loss. This option provides the highest earning potential for partners whose clients trade primarily on DTrader, SmartTrader, or BinaryBot.

When do I get paid my commissions?

Commissions are calculated daily and processed for payment weekly, provided you've reached the minimum withdrawal threshold of $50. Payments are typically processed on Mondays for the previous week's activity.

You can track pending and processed payments in real-time through your partner dashboard under the "Payments" section.

How do I track which commission model is earning me more?

Your partner dashboard provides detailed analytics that break down earnings by commission type, product, and client. This allows you to compare performance and optimize your strategy.

We recommend testing different approaches with distinct tracking links to determine which models work best for your audience.

Can I change my commission model after signing up?

Yes, you can adjust your preferred commission model for options trading through your partner dashboard. For CFD trading, turnover commission is the only available model.

Note that commission model changes may take up to 24 hours to take effect, and will only apply to new clients acquired after the change.

How do tier multipliers affect my commissions?

As you progress through our partner tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum), you earn multipliers that increase your base commission rates. For example, reaching Silver tier provides a 5% increase on your base commission rate.

Tier promotions are based on total commission earned and happen automatically when you reach the qualifying threshold.
