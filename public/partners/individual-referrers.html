Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn as an Individual Referrer?

Your earnings depend on how many clients you refer and their trading activity. Our most active Individual Referrers earn between $300-$1000 per month, but earnings can go much higher as you refer more clients.

Do I need trading knowledge to be successful?

No, you don't need any specialized trading knowledge. You simply share your referral links, and our platform and resources do the rest. However, having some basic understanding of trading can help you better engage potential clients.

How soon can I start earning?

You can start earning as soon as your referred clients begin trading. With the CPA plan, you'll earn after clients meet simple qualification requirements. With the Turnover plan, you'll earn based on clients' trading volume.

What promotional materials are available?

We provide a variety of ready-to-use materials including banners, landing pages, email templates, social media posts, and educational content you can share with your audience.

How do I get paid?

We offer multiple payment methods including bank transfers, e-wallets, and cryptocurrencies. Commissions are calculated daily and paid monthly, with a minimum withdrawal amount of just $50.
