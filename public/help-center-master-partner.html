Master Partner Program

The Master Partner Program offers an additional revenue stream by allowing you to build a network of sub-partners. This program enables you to expand your earnings potential beyond your direct client commissions.

Open to all partners: The Master Partner Program is available to all partners, regardless of your current tier or commission model.

How the Master Partner Program Works

The Master Partner Program creates a multi-level income opportunity based on a simple concept:

  • You refer other partners to join the Deriv Partner Program
  • These become your "sub-partners" in the system
  • You earn 20% of the commissions generated by your sub-partners
  • These additional earnings are credited monthly to your account

How to Get Started as a Master Partner

  1. Register as a Deriv partner if you haven't already
  2. Access your Master Partner section in the dashboard
  3. Generate your Master Partner referral link to share with potential sub-partners
  4. Promote the Deriv Partner Program to potential sub-partners using your unique link
  5. Monitor your sub-partner network and commissions through your dashboard
Pro Tip: The best sub-partners are often individuals or businesses already in your network who have an audience interested in trading or financial services.

Commission Structure

The Master Partner Program offers a straightforward commission structure:

Your Role Commission Earned Payment Schedule
Master Partner 20% of all commissions earned by your sub-partners Monthly

For example, if your sub-partner earns $1,000 in commissions in a month, you would earn an additional $200 (20%) as the Master Partner.

Important Notes:

  • There is no limit to the number of sub-partners you can refer
  • Your sub-partners receive 100% of their regular commission rates - your 20% is paid by Deriv
  • Commissions are calculated based on the net commission earned by your sub-partners
  • Payment follows the same schedules and methods as your regular partner commissions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I be both a regular partner and a Master Partner?

Yes, you can earn commissions both from your direct clients and from your sub-partners simultaneously. This creates multiple revenue streams within the same partner account.

Do I need to reach a certain tier before becoming a Master Partner?

No, the Master Partner Program is open to all partners regardless of tier. You can start referring sub-partners immediately after your partner account is approved.

How do I track the performance of my sub-partners?

Your partner dashboard includes a dedicated section for monitoring your sub-partner network. Here, you can view the number of active sub-partners, their performance, and your earned commissions from each.

What support do sub-partners receive?

Sub-partners receive the same comprehensive support as all Deriv partners, including access to marketing materials, the Partner Academy, and our partner support team. As a Master Partner, you also have the opportunity to provide additional guidance and mentorship.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn through the Master Partner Program?

There is no cap on earnings through the Master Partner Program. Your earnings potential is directly related to the number and performance of your sub-partners.
