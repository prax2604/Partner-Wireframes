Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Revenue Share commission calculated?

Revenue share commission is based on the net revenue generated by your clients' trading activity. The percentage depends on your partner tier:

  • Standard Tier (monthly net revenue $0-$20,000): 30% commission
  • Premium Tier (monthly net revenue >$20,000): 45% commission

The formula is: Client Net Revenue × Commission Rate = Your Commission

How is the Turnover Commission calculated?

Turnover commission is based on the total trading volume of your clients. The rates vary by product:

  • MT5 Standard: 10 pips commission
  • MT5 Advanced: 8 pips commission
  • MT5 Synthetic: 5% commission
  • Deriv X: 12 pips commission
  • Deriv EZ: 3% commission

For pip-based products, the formula is: (Trading Volume × Pips) / 100 = Your Commission

For percentage-based products, the formula is: Trading Volume × Commission Rate = Your Commission

How is the CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) calculated?

The CPA model provides a fixed payment for each qualified client you refer who meets specific requirements:

  • Basic qualification: Client deposits minimum amount and makes at least one trade
  • CPA rates range from $100 to $300 per qualified client depending on the region and product

The formula is: Number of Qualified Clients × CPA Rate = Your Commission

For this calculator, we use an average CPA rate of $150 per qualified client, with an estimated qualification rate of 60% for new clients.

Which commission model should I choose?

The best commission model depends on your business strategy and client base:

  • Revenue Share: Best for partners with active traders who generate consistent profits for Deriv
  • Turnover Commission: Ideal for partners with clients who trade frequently with high volumes
  • CPA: Good for partners who focus on client acquisition and prefer immediate fixed payments

You can also discuss with your account manager to find the best model for your specific situation or to create a hybrid model.
