Frequently Asked Questions

  • What partnership programmes does Deriv have?

    Deriv has the following partnership programmes available:

    • Affiliate & IB Programme: Introduce your clients to Deriv and earn up to 45% in commissions when they trade.
    • Deriv API: Create apps with our API and earn up to 5% in markup commissions.
    • Deriv Prime: Data-driven liquidity solutions designed for startup brokers.
    Learn more about Deriv partnership programmes ‚Üí
  • How do I become an Affiliate or an Introducing Broker (IB) with Deriv?

    To become a Deriv Affiliate:

    • Go to our Partners page and click on "Join Now".
    • Complete the Affiliate application form.
    • Wait for approval, which typically takes 1-3 working days.

    The approval process may take less time if you have a real Deriv account with the same email as your partner account application.

    If you have clients trading CFDs, you'll need to create a real Deriv MT5 Standard account to become an Introducing Broker (IB).

    View detailed application steps ‚Üí
  • Who is eligible to become a Deriv Affiliate?

    To be eligible as a Deriv Affiliate, you must:

    • Be 18 years old or older
    • Not reside in a FATF-restricted country

    Anyone capable of referring traders is welcome to apply.

    If you are any of the following, you'll have an even better chance of referring potential clients:

    • Trading experts
    • Content creators (bloggers, YouTubers, social media influencers)
    • Software developers
    Check complete eligibility criteria ‚Üí
  • Is there a fee to join the Deriv partnership programmes?

    There is no fee to join any of Deriv partnership programmes. It is free of charge and does not involve any hidden costs.

  • When and how do I get my commissions?

    You'll receive your commissions around the 15th of each month. It will be directly deposited into your selected payment method.

    If your clients are trading on Deriv MT5 accounts, commissions on those trades will be credited daily to your Deriv MT5 Standard account.

    To ensure you receive your commissions on time, set up your payment method and verify your payment threshold.

    Learn more about commission payments ‚Üí
  • How do I get my Deriv affiliate referral link?
    1. Log in to your affiliate dashboard.
    2. Click "Marketing" tab.
    3. Scroll down, select 'Direct links' on the left-hand side of your screen.
    4. Click on the link titled 'Direct signup- EN'.
    5. Copy the link under 'Landing page URL' and share it with your clients.

    You also may provide other referral links based on your clients' preferred languages.
