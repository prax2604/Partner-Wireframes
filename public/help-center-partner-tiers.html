Frequently Asked Questions

How is my tier calculated?

Your tier is calculated based on your monthly commission earnings across all commission models (Revenue Share, Turnover, and CPA). Once you reach a threshold, you'll be automatically upgraded to the corresponding tier.

Can I be downgraded to a lower tier?

Yes, if your commission earnings fall below the threshold for your current tier for three consecutive months, your tier may be adjusted accordingly. However, we provide a grace period and notification before any downgrades occur.

How do commission multipliers work?

Commission multipliers increase your base commission rates by a percentage. For example, as a Gold Partner with a 110% multiplier, if the standard commission for a specific trading product is $10, you would earn $11 (10% more) for the same client activity.

What is the Partner 121 program?

The Partner 121 program is an exclusive mentorship opportunity available only to VIP partners. It pairs you with a senior partner manager who provides personalized guidance, strategy development, and business planning to maximize your success as a partner.

How can I reach the VIP tier?

The VIP tier is by invitation only and considers factors beyond just commission levels, including long-term partnership commitment, business alignment, and strategic growth potential. Consistently high performance at the Platinum tier is typically a prerequisite for VIP consideration.

Tips for Advancing Through Tiers

  1. Focus on client quality - Clients who are active traders generate more commissions than inactive ones
  2. Diversify your marketing channels - Utilize multiple platforms to reach potential clients
  3. Leverage the Partner Academy - Learn advanced strategies to optimize your conversions
  4. Utilize all available marketing materials - Take advantage of the resources provided for each tier
  5. Consider the Master Partner Program - Earning from sub-partners can help boost your commission totals
