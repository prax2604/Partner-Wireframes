Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my contact information?

To update your contact information:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners dashboard
2. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
3. Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu
4. Navigate to the "Contact Information" section
5. Update your email, phone number, or address as needed
6. Click "Save Changes" to apply your updates

Note: Changing your email address will require verification of the new email.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you can easily reset it by following these steps:

1. Go to the Deriv Partners login page
2. Click on the "Forgot password?" link below the login button
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact partner support for assistance.

How do I set up two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Setting up 2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account. Here's how to enable it:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners dashboard
2. Go to "Account Settings" > "Security"
3. Click "Enable" next to Two-Factor Authentication
4. Download an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator or Authy) if you don't have one
5. Scan the QR code displayed on the screen with your authenticator app
6. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app to verify setup
7. Save your backup codes in a secure location

Once enabled, you'll need to enter a code from your authenticator app each time you log in, in addition to your password.

How can I change my commission payment method?

To update your commission payment method:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners dashboard
2. Navigate to "Account Settings" > "Payment Information"
3. Click "Edit Payment Method"
4. Select your preferred payment method from the available options
5. Enter the required payment details
6. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Changes to payment methods may take up to 24 hours to be processed. If you're updating your payment method close to a payment date, the change might only apply to future payments.

Can I have multiple users on my partner account?

Yes, if you have a business partner account, you can add multiple users with different permission levels. To add users:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners dashboard
2. Go to "Account Settings" > "User Management"
3. Click "Add New User"
4. Enter the user's email address and set their permission level
5. Click "Send Invitation"

The invited user will receive an email with instructions to create their own login credentials. As the account owner, you can modify or revoke access for any user at any time.
