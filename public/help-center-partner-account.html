Creating Your Partner Account

Setting up your Deriv partner account is quick and straightforward. This guide will walk you through the registration process step by step, ensuring you have everything you need to get started as a Deriv partner.

Before you begin: Have your identification documents and proof of address ready for the verification process.

Registration Process

1

Visit the Deriv Partners Registration Page

Go to the Deriv Partners website and click on "Sign Up" button.

Sign up now
2

Complete the Registration Form

Fill out the registration form with your personal or business information. All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.

Sample Registration Form

3

Choose Your Partnership Type

Select the partnership model that best suits your business needs:

  • Affiliate: For website owners, social media influencers, and marketers who can promote Deriv products.
  • Introducing Broker (IB): For individuals or companies that can introduce traders to Deriv platforms.
  • API Partner: For developers who can create trading applications using Deriv API.

Not sure which type is right for you? Learn more about partnership types.

4

Verify Your Account

To comply with regulatory requirements and ensure security, you'll need to verify your account by providing:

  • Proof of Identity: A valid government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Proof of Address: A recent utility bill, bank statement, or official letter (issued within the last 6 months)
  • Business Documentation: If you're registering as a business, you'll need additional documents such as business registration certificates
Tip: Make sure your documents are clear, legible, and show all four corners of the document for faster verification.
5

Submit Your Application

Review all your information, accept the terms and conditions, and submit your application. Our team will review your application within 1-3 business days.

You'll receive an email notification once your account has been approved or if we need additional information.

After Registration

Once your account is approved, you can:

  • Log in to your partner dashboard
  • Select your preferred commission model
  • Generate tracking links and promotional materials
  • Set up your payment details to receive commissions
Need help with registration? Contact our partner support team at partners@deriv.com for assistance.
