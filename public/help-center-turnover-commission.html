Turnover Commission Model

The Turnover Commission model is a popular commission structure that rewards you based on the volume (turnover) of trades placed by your clients, rather than on Deriv's revenue from those trades. This model offers consistent and predictable earnings, making it ideal for partners who bring high trading volumes.

Important: Turnover Commission applies to both CFD and derivatives products, giving you broader earning potential across the full range of Deriv's trading offerings.

Key Benefits of Turnover Commission

Predictable Earnings

Earn a fixed percentage of all trading volume from your referred clients, regardless of whether those trades result in profits or losses for Deriv.

Wide Product Coverage

Earn commissions on both CFD trading (MT5, Deriv X, cTrader) and derivatives trading (DTrader, Deriv GO, SmartTrader).

Volume-Based Rewards

The more your clients trade, the more you earn, making this model ideal for partners who focus on high-volume traders.

How Turnover Commission Works

With the Turnover Commission model, you earn a fixed percentage of the total volume traded by your referred clients. Here's a simplified overview of how it works:

  1. You refer clients to Deriv through your affiliate links
  2. Clients register and start trading on Deriv platforms
  3. You earn a commission based on the total volume traded by your clients
  4. Commission rates vary based on your partner tier and the product type
Note: Turnover Commission is calculated on the volume of trades, not on the deposits or withdrawals made by clients.

Calculation Examples

Example 1: Forex Trading

Scenario: Your clients trade 300 lots of EUR/USD (a major forex pair) in a month, and you're on the Gold tier with a rate of 0.9 pips.

Calculation:

  • 300 lots × 100,000 units = $30,000,000 volume
  • $30,000,000 × 0.9 pips (0.00009) = $2,700

Your commission for that month would be $2,700.

Example 2: Cryptocurrency Trading

Scenario: Your clients trade $500,000 volume of Bitcoin CFDs in a month, and you're on the Standard tier with a rate of 0.3%.

Calculation:

  • $500,000 × 0.3% = $1,500

Your commission for that month would be $1,500.

Example 3: Derivatives Trading

Scenario: Your clients place $200,000 in multiplier trades in a month, and you're on the Silver tier with a rate of 0.7%.

Calculation:

  • $200,000 × 0.7% = $1,400

Your commission for that month would be $1,400.

Comparison with Other Models

Different commission models suit different partner strategies and client types:

Turnover vs. Revenue Share

  • Turnover: Based on trading volume regardless of outcomes; more stable earnings.
  • Revenue Share: Based on company revenue; potentially higher earnings with derivatives traders.

Turnover vs. CPA

  • Turnover: Ongoing commissions based on trading volume; unlimited earning potential.
  • CPA: One-time payment for client acquisition; simpler but limited earning potential.
