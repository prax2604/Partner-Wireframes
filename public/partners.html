COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about our partnership program

How do I get started as a Deriv partner?

Starting is simple: Create your free account, complete your profile, and get your unique referral links. You can begin sharing these links immediately to start earning commissions. We provide all the marketing materials and support you need to succeed.

Do I need trading experience to become a partner?

No, trading experience is not required. Our program is designed for anyone who can refer clients, whether you're a blogger, content creator, community leader, or just someone with a network. We provide comprehensive training and resources to help you understand our products sufficiently for marketing purposes.

How and when do I get paid?

We process payments weekly for all partners who have reached the minimum withdrawal threshold of $50. You can choose from multiple payment methods including bank transfers, e-wallets, and cryptocurrencies. Detailed transaction reports are available in your partner dashboard.

What marketing materials do you provide?

We offer a comprehensive range of marketing resources including banners, landing pages, videos, email templates, and social media content in multiple languages. Our team can also create custom materials for high-volume partners and provide guidance on effective marketing strategies.

What commission rates do you offer?

We offer competitive commission structures with multiple plans to choose from. Our revenue share plan offers up to 45% of the net revenue generated by your clients. As you advance through our partner tiers, you'll receive multipliers that further increase your commission rates.

Can I become a partner if my country has restrictions?

While we operate globally, there are certain restrictions based on regulatory requirements. Please contact our partnership team to discuss your specific situation. We're constantly expanding our jurisdictional coverage and may be able to recommend alternative solutions.
