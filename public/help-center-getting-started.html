Understanding the Partner Journey

Your journey as a Deriv partner follows a clear progression, with each stage building on the previous one. Let's explore each stage in detail:

Stage 1: Discovery

The discovery stage is where you are now - learning about Deriv's products, partnership options, and exploring the potential opportunities. During this stage, you'll:

  • Learn about Deriv's trading products and platforms
  • Understand our commission structures and partnership models
  • Explore the Partner Academy resources
  • Identify which partnership approach aligns with your goals

Stage 2: Activation

During the activation stage, you'll complete your registration and set up your partner account. Key activities include:

  • Completing the one-step registration process
  • Selecting your preferred commission model for Options products
  • Setting up your payment details
  • Creating your first tracking links for promotion
  • Exploring the partner dashboard and its features
1

Complete Your Registration

The first practical step is to complete our simplified registration process:

  1. Visit deriv.com/partners and click "Sign up"
  2. Fill in your personal and business information
  3. Select your preferred commission model for Options products (Revenue Share or Turnover)
  4. Submit your application for approval

Your account will typically be approved within 1-2 business days.

2

Access Your Partner Dashboard

Once approved, you'll gain access to your unified partner dashboard where you can:

  • Find tracking links for both CFD and Options products in one place
  • Access marketing materials for all Deriv products
  • Set up your payment details for commission payouts
  • Review performance tracking tools that monitor all your client activity

Watch a video tutorial of the dashboard

3

Create Your First Tracking Links

Generate your first set of tracking links to begin promoting Deriv:

  1. Log in to your partner dashboard
  2. Navigate to the "Marketing Tools" section
  3. Select "Tracking Links" from the menu
  4. Choose to create links for all Deriv products or specific platforms
  5. Customize parameters for better tracking (optional)
  6. Generate and copy your unique tracking links

Remember: You don't need to create separate campaigns for CFD and Options - you can promote all products together with a single link.

Stage 3: First Success

This critical stage focuses on acquiring your first clients and earning your initial commissions. During this stage, you'll:

  • Begin sharing your tracking links through your preferred channels
  • Implement basic promotional strategies
  • Track your first client registrations and deposits
  • Receive your first commission payments
  • Learn from initial results to improve your approach

Stage 4: Growth

With your first success achieved, the growth stage is about scaling your partner business. Key activities include:

  • Expanding your promotional channels and reach
  • Optimizing conversion rates through A/B testing
  • Building a more substantial client base
  • Moving up through partner tiers to earn bonus commissions
  • Exploring additional revenue streams (like the Master Partner program)

Stage 5: Mastery

The final stage represents mastery of the partner business model. At this stage, you'll:

  • Implement advanced marketing and acquisition strategies
  • Potentially develop a network of sub-partners
  • Create systematic approaches to client acquisition and retention
  • Reach higher partner tiers with premium benefits
  • Possibly mentor newer partners through your expertise
Partner Tip: While the journey is presented as sequential stages, many partners find themselves working on elements from multiple stages simultaneously. Focus on mastering the basics before moving to more advanced strategies.
