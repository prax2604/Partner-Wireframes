Getting Started with Deriv Partners

Welcome to the Deriv Partners program! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start your journey as a Deriv partner and help you maximize your earning potential.

New to Deriv Partners? Follow this step-by-step guide to get your partnership up and running quickly.

Your Partnership Journey

1

Sign up for a Deriv partner account

Create your free partner account by completing the registration form. You'll need to provide basic information about yourself or your business.

Register now
2

Choose your partnership type

Select the partnership model that best fits your business needs:

  • Affiliate: Promote Deriv products and earn commissions for client referrals
  • IB (Introducing Broker): Introduce clients to trade on Deriv and earn from their trading activity
  • API partner: Build trading applications using our API and earn from client trading
3

Complete verification

Submit the required documentation to verify your account. This typically includes:

  • Proof of identity
  • Proof of address
  • Business documentation (for company accounts)

Our team will review your application within 1-3 business days.

4

Access your partner dashboard

Once approved, you'll gain access to your personalized dashboard where you can:

  • Generate tracking links and marketing materials
  • Monitor client activity and commissions
  • Access detailed performance reports
  • Manage your payment details
5

Start promoting Deriv

Use our marketing tools and resources to start promoting Deriv products to your audience:

  • Implement your tracking links on your website, blog, or social media
  • Utilize our banners, landing pages, and other marketing materials
  • Share your unique referral link with potential clients

Commission Models

As a Deriv partner, understanding the available commission models is essential for maximizing your earnings. Visit our Commission Models page for detailed information.

We offer three main commission models:

Commission Model How It Works Best For
Turnover Commission Earn based on the trading volume of your clients on both CFD and derivatives products Partners with CFD traders or high-volume traders
Revenue Share Earn a percentage of the revenue generated by your referred clients on derivatives products Partners with clients who frequently trade derivatives
CPA (EU Only) Receive a one-time payment for each qualified client you refer from EU countries Partners focusing on EU markets
Pro Tip: You can use our Commission Calculator to estimate your potential earnings under different models.
