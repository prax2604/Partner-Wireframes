Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I change my commission model after signing up?

    Yes, you can change your commission model once per month by submitting a request through your partner dashboard. The change will take effect from the 1st day of the following month. Note that some historical data might not be transferred between models.

    Learn more about changing models ‚Üí
  • Which commission model is best for CFD trading clients?

    For partners with clients who primarily trade CFDs on MT5, the turnover model is typically the most advantageous. This model pays a fixed commission per lot traded, regardless of whether clients win or lose, ensuring steady income based on trading volume.

    Learn more about CFD commissions ‚Üí
  • How do partner tiers affect my commission rates?

    Higher partner tiers can increase your commission rates by 5-10% depending on the tier level. For example, a Diamond tier partner could earn up to 45% revenue share instead of the standard 40%. Tier status is determined by the number of active clients and total trading volume over a 3-month period.

    Learn more about partner tiers ‚Üí
  • Is there a minimum payout threshold for commissions?

    Yes, there is a minimum payout threshold of $50 (or equivalent in other currencies). If your commission total is below this threshold, it will be carried forward to the next payment period until the minimum is reached.

    Learn more about payment thresholds ‚Üí
  • How long do clients remain attributed to my account?

    Clients remain attributed to your account indefinitely, as long as they continue to use your referral link or sign up code. There is no expiration date for client attribution, ensuring you earn commissions from their activity for as long as they remain active traders.
