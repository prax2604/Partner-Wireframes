Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track client registrations from my referral links?

You can track client registrations from your referral links through the Deriv Partners dashboard:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners account
2. Navigate to the "Reports" section
3. Select "Client Acquisition" from the report types
4. Filter by date range or specific tracking link
5. View detailed information about clients who registered using your links

The report includes registration dates, client IDs, and the specific tracking links used for registration. You can export this data as CSV or Excel for further analysis.

What information can I see about my referred clients?

For compliance and privacy reasons, you can view performance data without accessing personal client information. Available data includes:

- Client ID (anonymized)
- Registration date
- Country of residence
- Trading activity summary
- Deposit and withdrawal summaries
- Account status (active, dormant, closed)
- Generated commissions

This information allows you to analyze client performance while respecting privacy regulations and data protection standards.

How can I create multiple tracking links for different campaigns?

To create multiple tracking links for different campaigns:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners dashboard
2. Go to the "Marketing Tools" section
3. Select "Tracking Links"
4. Click the "Create New Link" button
5. Choose your campaign type (social media, website, email, etc.)
6. Add a unique campaign name to easily identify this specific tracking link
7. Select your target landing page
8. Click "Generate Link"

You can create up to 100 unique tracking links for different campaigns, allowing you to measure the effectiveness of each marketing channel separately.

How do I identify my best-performing clients?

To identify your best-performing clients:

1. Log in to your Deriv Partners dashboard
2. Navigate to the "Reports" section
3. Select "Client Performance" from the report types
4. Filter the report by date range (e.g., last 30 days, last quarter)
5. Click on the "Sort By" option and select "Commissions Generated" or "Trading Volume"
6. Review the sorted list of clients with the highest revenue generation

Additionally, you can leverage the Analytics Dashboard to visualize client performance patterns and identify trends in your top-performing clients, which can help inform your client acquisition and retention strategies.

Can I see which marketing materials are most effective?

Yes, you can track the effectiveness of different marketing materials:

1. Create specific tracking links for each marketing material
2. Access the "Campaign Performance" report in your dashboard
3. Compare metrics such as click-through rates, conversion rates, and commissions generated by each link
4. View the performance data by campaign type, timeframe, or geographic region

This information helps you understand which materials drive the most registrations, deposits, and active trading, allowing you to optimize your marketing efforts and focus on the most effective channels and content.
